Overview

This page explains how users of the Overcome: Dopamine Reset for Men Android app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Account deletion is processed by email. There is no waiting period — submit your request and we will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

Your privacy matters to us. All personal data associated with your account is permanently deleted upon request, with the limited exceptions described below required by Indian law.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion in two ways:

Option 1 — From inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Overcome app and go to your Profile Page.
  2. Tap "Request Account Deletion". This opens a pre-filled email addressed to overcomeapp0@gmail.com.
  3. Send the email without modifying the subject line.
  4. We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

Option 2 — By email directly

Send your request to
overcomeapp0@gmail.com
Subject line: "Data Deletion Request" — include the email address associated with your account

Upon receiving your request we will send a confirmation email. Your account and all associated data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days of that confirmation.

A limited amount of data must be retained after account deletion as required by Indian law. This data cannot be deleted on request.
