3. What Counts as a Completed Week

A "completed week" is a week in which every assigned challenge has met its individual daily threshold as automatically recorded in the app.

Different challenges have different thresholds — some require completion on 3 out of 7 days, some on 4 out of 7, and some on 5 out of 7. The threshold for each challenge is displayed clearly within the app.

A week is only complete when every challenge in that week has individually met its threshold. Partially completing some challenges does not count as a completed week.

Tier 3 surprise challenges are excluded from the weekly completion calculation. Only Tier 1 and Tier 2 challenges count toward guarantee eligibility. Tier 3 challenges disappear on the day they appear and are not tracked for completion purposes.

Completion is determined solely by in-app logs. No screenshots, external records, or manual submissions are accepted. Completion logs are immutable — they cannot be retroactively edited by users or administrators.

5. What Disqualifies a Claim

A cashback claim will be rejected if any of the following conditions apply:

  • All required weeks were not completed — 13/13 for The 90-Day Reset, 26/26 for The 180-Day Full Reset
  • The claim was submitted outside the 14-day claim window after the guarantee period ended
  • Challenge completion data was found to be falsified, manipulated, or artificially inflated
  • Multiple accounts were created to circumvent the guarantee
  • The user was in breach of the Terms of Service at the time of the claim
  • A chargeback was already filed with Google Play or Apple App Store for the same subscription period
  • More than one claim was submitted in the same subscription period
  • The subscription was cancelled before the plan ran to its full end date

6. What Is Not Covered

The following are not eligible for a refund or cashback under any circumstances:

  • Subscriptions cancelled mid-period where the plan did not run to its full end date
  • The 10% retained from any approved cashback claim — this is non-refundable
  • Subscription periods where a chargeback has already been initiated via Google Play or Apple App Store
  • Requests made after the 14-day claim window has closed, regardless of reason
  • Claims based on dissatisfaction with individual features rather than overall programme results
Overcome does not offer no-questions-asked refunds. If you are unsure about committing to a plan, we recommend starting with The 90-Day Reset, which has a shorter commitment period and the same guarantee terms.

7. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your app store subscription settings. Cancellation prevents future auto-renewal but does not entitle you to a refund for the current billing period.

  • Android: Google Play Store > Account > Subscriptions > Overcome > Cancel
  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Overcome > Cancel

Cancelling your subscription also disqualifies you from the Cashback Guarantee for that subscription period, as the plan must run to its full end date to be eligible.
