Before you begin — please read this.
This isn't your usual disclaimer. It's worth 60 seconds.

You're here because you want to change something real about your life. That takes courage, and we don't take that lightly.

Overcome is built to support that change — through structure, daily challenges, and honest progress tracking. But we want to be clear about what we are and what we're not.

What Overcome is:

A habit-building tool designed to help you reduce compulsive behaviour and rebuild focus — one day at a time.

What Overcome is not:

A medical service, therapy platform, or clinical treatment of any kind. Nothing in this app constitutes medical advice. We do not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition or disorder.

If you're going through something serious — depression, anxiety, or anything that feels bigger than a habit — please speak with a qualified mental health professional. Don't rely on an app when you need real support.
