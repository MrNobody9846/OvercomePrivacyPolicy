Overcome is a habit-building and dopamine reset mobile application developed and operated by its co-founders as independent developers based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. We are not a registered company at this time. In this document, "we", "us", and "our" refer to the Overcome development team.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Overcome experience. Below is a complete account of every type of data we collect and the specific reason for collecting it.

2.1 Data You Provide at Account Creation

Data TypePurpose
Email addressTo create and manage your account, send important service communications, and process cashback claims.
Display nameTo personalise your in-app experience. Can be set to any name — does not need to be your real name.
PasswordTo secure your account. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption and are never stored in plain text. Credential management is handled by Descope.

2.2 Data You Provide During Onboarding

Data TypePurpose
Current streak (days)Self-reported. Used to establish your baseline and personalise your starting point in the programme.
Longest streak ever (days)Self-reported. Used to understand your history and set appropriate programme targets.
Primary trigger typeSelected from a fixed list of categories provided in the app. Used to tailor challenge recommendations to your specific patterns.
Primary goalSelected from a fixed list. Used to personalise your programme structure.

2.3 Data Generated During App Use

Data TypePurpose
Daily challenge completion logsRecorded automatically per challenge per day. Stored to track your progress, calculate your Clarity Score, and verify cashback guarantee eligibility. Completion logs are immutable — they cannot be retroactively edited.
Streak dataCurrent streak, longest streak, and relapse log dates. Core programme tracking data.
Relapse logsDate and dominant emotion selected at time of logging. Optional — logging is encouraged but not required. Used for self-reflection and Clarity Score depth component.
Clarity Score historyDaily snapshots of your composite progress score. Used to display your progress over time.
Wellbeing check-in ratingsOptional daily 1–5 self-rating of energy and clarity. Feeds the Clarity Score depth component.
Badge earn dates and IDsRecords of which achievement badges you have earned and when. Used to display your badge collection.
App open timestamps and session frequencyUsed to calculate the consistency component of your Clarity Score and to improve the app experience.
Device type and OS versionCollected automatically for crash reporting and app compatibility purposes.

2.4 Data Collected via Third-Party Services

Data TypePurpose
Authentication data (Descope)Your email address, display name, and hashed password are processed by Descope at account creation and on each login. Used for identity verification and secure account access only.
Subscription status and transaction ID (Google Play)Collected when you purchase a subscription via Google Play. Used to manage your plan access.
Subscription status and transaction ID (RevenueCat)RevenueCat manages subscription state on our behalf. It receives your subscription status, transaction IDs, and an anonymised app user ID.
Anonymous usage events and crash data (Firebase)Collected by Google Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. Includes anonymised screen views, session data, and error logs.

We never collect your precise location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or any data unrelated to your use of Overcome.
